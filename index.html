Information stored only on your device is not automatically transmitted to XenDevApp.

You can remove locally stored information by using any available reset or clear-data option in the App, clearing the App’s storage through your device settings, or uninstalling the App.

5. AI Conversation Processing

Marlow includes an AI bartender feature. When you submit a message or request an AI-generated recommendation, the information required to generate the response may be transmitted to an AI service provider.

The information transmitted may include:

  • Your message or question
  • The ingredients you choose to include in your request
  • Your selected cocktail or taste preferences
  • Relevant previous messages required to understand the current conversation

This information is processed only to understand your request, generate a response, operate the AI feature, maintain security, and prevent misuse.

Do not enter confidential information or highly sensitive personal information into the AI chat. This includes financial information, passwords, government identification numbers, medical records, or information about another person.

AI-generated content may occasionally be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable. Recipes and recommendations should be reviewed before use, particularly where allergies, dietary restrictions, medication interactions, pregnancy, health conditions, or alcohol sensitivity may be relevant.
